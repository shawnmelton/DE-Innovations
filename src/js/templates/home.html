 Solution Dominion Enterprises can combat these challenges starting with a creative space that fosters a healthy, productive environment.
Collaboration Creativity Celebration Culture  Beyond Space The creative space is a great idea, but with a few additional perks it can become an amazing tool for our company.

Providing time and space for our brightest and most motivated workers to come together to find the best solutions creates a more engaging and fulfilling work environment.

The synergy found in a space dedicated focused and even out-of-the box thinking is not easily manufactured. A space that provides the tools necessary for meaningful collaboration will.

We have achieved so much in the past few years. Let’s build a culture of regular celebration where we highlight the great things we’ve done and appreciate the talent around us.

Culture is never made by policy. Culture is made by people. Still, it is important that policy not stand in the way of people creating a culture of pride, ownership, innovation, and success.

Creative Space

The DE creative space is where engineers, designers, and knowledge workers come together to tackle challenges and develop the next wave of big ideas. They collaborate at the whiteboards around the space. Developers and designers find a spot at a standing desks to write code and develop experiences. This is the space where innovation is cultivated and facilitated in a way that the typical office layout simply can’t afford.

Morning Meetups

Adding a place for Dominion Enterprise employees to meet in the morning will foster collaboration and idea sharing. Providing coffee and a comfortable setting would be an attractive destination for before and after work social gatherings.

Idea Board

Adding an idea board would provide a location for employees to talk about the big challenges that our companies are trying to overcome. Pulling in innovative, creative thinking during lunch breaks (or early mornings) would allow companies to benefit from thought leaders that are typically unavailable when companies work in their own siloes.

Innovation Reel

Adding an innovation reel on a marketing site that flaunts our innovation and creative space would bolster our recruiting efforts. This beautiful web site would visually show candidates what recruiters and hiring managers have been attempting to convey orally.

Incubator

Adding an incubator would allow Dominion Enterprises to foster excellent ideas and watch them grow into full-fledged solutions. Hackathon winners could receive an additional week, two weeks or a month based on the merit of their efforts. Companies would reap the rewards of seeing their employee's ideas become reality.
